The Poe Foundation: A family tradition of giving back.

About Us

The Poe Foundation is committed to strengthening our community by supporting causes that align with our family’s core values of education, health, preservation, community development, and the arts.

Vision: A legacy of giving that uplifts generations.

Values: Integrity, Stewardship, Community, Family Legacy

Our Initiatives

  • Education Support – scholarships and school partnerships
  • Community Health – bringing awareness to health and disabilities
  • Arts & Culture – local grants and museum support

Our Story

Founded by the Poe family, our foundation carries forward a legacy of compassion and community service. From humble beginnings to generational impact, this is our family's journey of giving.
